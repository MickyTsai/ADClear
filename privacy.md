# ADClear Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-06-16

ADClear respects your privacy. ADClear does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

ADClear is a Safari Content Blocker. The app downloads public filter lists, such as EasyList, and converts them locally on your device into Safari content blocker rules.

ADClear does not know, record, or transmit the websites you visit in Safari. Safari applies the content blocker rules on device.

## Data Collection

ADClear does not collect:

- Browsing history
- Search history
- Website content
- Personal identifiers
- Location data
- Analytics data
- Advertising identifiers

## Network Access

ADClear may connect to public filter list providers, such as EasyList, only when updating blocking rules. This request is used to download public filter rules and is not used to identify users.

## Third Parties

ADClear uses public filter lists and open source libraries to provide content blocking functionality. ADClear does not share user data with these providers.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Because ADClear does not collect personal data, there is no personal data retained by ADClear and no personal data to delete.

# ADClear 隱私權政策

生效日期：2026-06-16

ADClear 重視你的隱私。ADClear 不會收集、儲存、販售或分享任何個人資料。

ADClear 是 Safari 內容阻擋器。App 會下載公開的封鎖規則清單，例如 EasyList，並在你的裝置本機轉換為 Safari Content Blocker 規則。

ADClear 不會知道、記錄或傳送你在 Safari 瀏覽的網站。Safari 會在裝置本機套用內容阻擋規則。

## 資料收集

ADClear 不會收集：

- 瀏覽紀錄
- 搜尋紀錄
- 網站內容
- 個人識別資訊
- 位置資料
- 分析資料
- 廣告識別碼

## 網路連線

ADClear 只會在更新封鎖規則時連線至公開規則清單提供者，例如 EasyList。此連線僅用於下載公開的封鎖規則，不會用於識別使用者。

## 第三方

ADClear 使用公開規則清單與開源函式庫提供內容阻擋功能。ADClear 不會與這些提供者分享使用者資料。

## 資料保留與刪除

由於 ADClear 不會收集個人資料，因此 ADClear 不會保留任何個人資料，也沒有個人資料需要刪除。
